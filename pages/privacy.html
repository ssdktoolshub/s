Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is SSDK Tools Hub's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Most of the tools provided on SSDK Tools Hub run entirely on client-side technology (directly in your browser using JavaScript). We do not upload your text logs, images, code structures, documents, or personal credentials to any database. Any operations you perform remain local to your own device.

2. Account and Database Information

If you optionally create an account, we use Firebase Auth to store your email, username, and password credentials securely. We use Firestore to save your list of favorite tool names and dashboard reviews. This data is kept secure and is never shared, rented, or sold to third-party marketing services.

3. Browser Cookies & LocalStorage

We use local storage (LocalStorage) on your device to remember user preferences (such as light or dark theme choice). We do not track browser histories or session clicks outside of basic, anonymous analytics logs used to monitor site performance.

4. Third-Party Connections

Our website may link to tools, resources, or services hosted on external hosting platforms (like Render or Vercel). We do not control the content or policies of these external sites and cannot accept liability for their respective privacy practices.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us through our dedicated Contact Page.
