📚 Clinical Pathology Notes: TIBC & Saturation

What is TIBC?
Total Iron Binding Capacity (TIBC) measures the maximum amount of iron that can bind to transferrin (the primary iron-transporting protein). It serves as an indirect gauge of transferrin concentration.

What is Transferrin Saturation?
Transferrin Saturation (TSAT) indicates the percentage of transferrin binding sites occupied by iron. A low TSAT (< 20%) points towards iron deficiency anemia, while a high TSAT (> 50%) suggests iron overload or hemochromatosis.
