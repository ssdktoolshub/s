Frequently Asked Questions

How do I specify splits?＋
Enter page numbers separated by commas, or ranges using dashes. For example, entering 1-3, 5 will extract pages 1, 2, 3, and 5 into your new document.
Can this tool split password-protected PDFs?＋
No. For security reasons, encrypted or password-protected PDF files must be decrypted before processing. We do not support password cracking to guarantee serverless processing.
