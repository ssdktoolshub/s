Frequently Asked Questions

What are SEO keyword stop-words?＋
Stop-words are high-frequency grammatical linkers like "the", "and", "in", "is". Our density analyzer filters these out so you see actual topic keywords.
Does the reader work offline?＋
Yes. Text-to-Speech uses the browser's built-in SpeechSynthesis engine (Windows, macOS, Android, iOS) — no internet needed.
How does the highlighter work without breaking editing?＋
An invisible overlay mirrors your textarea content. Colored spans appear behind the text so your cursor and selection stay fully normal.
Is rephrase AI-powered?＋
No — it's 100% client-side. Suggestions use a built-in synonym map (50+ common words) and structural reversal — no data ever leaves your browser.
