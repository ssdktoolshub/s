📚 Clinical Pathology Notes: CK-MB

What is CK-MB?
Creatine Kinase-MB (CK-MB) is an isoenzyme of creatine kinase found primarily in cardiac muscle cells. Elevated levels in blood indicate cardiac muscle damage.

How does CK-MB compare to Troponin?
While cardiac Troponin is the preferred biomarker due to higher cardiac specificity, CK-MB is highly useful to evaluate re-infarction (second heart attack) because CK-MB levels return to baseline much quicker (within 48-72 hours) than Troponin.
