📚 Clinical Pathology Notes: Creatinine Clearance

What is Creatinine Clearance (CrCl)?
Creatinine Clearance estimates the rate at which kidneys clear creatinine from blood plasma. It is widely used in pharmacology to adjust dosages of medications cleared by the kidneys.

What is the Cockcroft-Gault equation?
The Cockcroft-Gault formula is:
CrCl (mL/min) = ((140 - Age) × Weight in kg) / (72 × Creatinine in mg/dL) (multiplied by 0.85 if female).
