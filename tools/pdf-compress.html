Frequently Asked Questions

Does local PDF compression affect image or text quality?＋
Our client-side compression reduces file sizes by removing duplicate structural streams, cleaning document revision histories, and stripping metadata. Since we do not perform lossy image down-sampling by default, your texts and graphics retain their crispness.
Why does my PDF compress very little?＋
If your PDF document is already highly optimized (such as a text-only PDF saved directly from modern word processors) or contains mostly flat, compressed image files, there will be very little metadata or redundant structure to prune, resulting in a small compression ratio.
