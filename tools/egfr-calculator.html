📚 Clinical Pathology Notes: eGFR & CKD Stages

What is eGFR?
eGFR (Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate) measures how much blood passes through the tiny kidney filters (glomeruli) each minute. It is adjusted for body surface area.

What is the 2021 CKD-EPI equation?
The 2021 CKD-EPI creatinine equation is a refit of the CKD-EPI equations, developed to eliminate race-based coefficients to improve clinical impartiality and diagnostic accuracy.
