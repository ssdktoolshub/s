Frequently Asked Questions

Is my spreadsheet data safe?＋
Yes. Your spreadsheets are processed locally inside your browser using JavaScript. No files are uploaded to our servers, ensuring your business data remains completely private.
What file size limits does it support?＋
Since calculations are performed client-side, the performance is limited by your computer's RAM and processor. Most browsers can comfortably handle sheets with tens of thousands of rows (files up to 20-30MB) without issues.
