GCS Score Injury Classification Clinical Indications
13 – 15 Mild Brain Injury Minor concussion, alert and oriented patient. High probability of recovery.
9 – 12 Moderate Brain Injury Significant trauma, confused state, lethargy. Watch closely for neurological decline.
3 – 8 Severe Brain Injury (Coma) Comatose patient, unconscious, high airway risk. Intubation typically indicated if score ≤ 8.
