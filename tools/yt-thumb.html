Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the HD thumbnail look blank or show a generic image?＋
YouTube only generates the Maximum Resolution (maxresdefault) cover if the video creator uploaded a high-definition custom thumbnail (1280x720 pixels or greater). If they didn't, YouTube displays a generic placeholder. In such cases, use the High Quality (HQ) or Medium (MQ) options.
Where are downloaded images saved?＋
Images are downloaded directly through your browser, usually saving to your system's default "Downloads" directory.
