Frequently Asked Questions

What is an XML Sitemap?＋
An XML sitemap is a file containing a list of all your website's primary URLs. It acts as a roadmap for search engines, telling crawlers which pages are available, when they were last modified, and their crawl priority.
Does a sitemap guarantee that all my pages will be indexed?＋
No. Sitemap submission informs search engine spiders of your site structure, making discovery much easier. However, whether a page actually gets indexed is determined by search engine algorithms based on content value, authority, and SEO design.
