Frequently Asked Questions

Where do meta tags go in my HTML code?＋
Meta tags must be placed inside the <head> block at the top of your HTML file, usually before any stylesheet imports or script references.
What are Open Graph tags?＋
Open Graph tags are meta tags defined by the Open Graph Protocol. They allow website owners to control how their web pages are formatted and displayed when shared on social networks like Facebook, LinkedIn, or Discord.
What are standard tag length limits?＋
Search engines typically show the first 50–60 characters of a title tag and 150–160 characters of a description tag. Keeping your metadata within these parameters ensures that your snippets do not get truncated (cut off) in search rankings.
