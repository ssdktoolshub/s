📚 Clinical Pathology Notes: Estimated Average Glucose

What is the ADAG equation?
The A1c-Derived Average Glucose (ADAG) mathematical relation is:
eAG (mg/dL) = (28.7 × HbA1c) - 46.7. This helps bridge laboratory average percentages with everyday diagnostic values.

How are units converted?
Average glucose is converted to mmol/L using the conversion factor: mmol/L = mg/dL ÷ 18.
