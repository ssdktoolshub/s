Frequently Asked Questions

Does cropping happen before resizing?＋
Yes. When you drag and draw the cropping grid, clicking Process & Export first clips the cropped selection, applies filters, and then scales the output to your target pixel width and height.
What quality setting should I use?＋
We recommend 80% quality for WebP and JPEG. It significantly reduces file storage bytes without any noticeable drop in image clarity.
