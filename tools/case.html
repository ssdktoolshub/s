Frequently Asked Questions

What is camelCase and PascalCase used for?＋
Both camelCase and PascalCase are variable naming styles used in programming where spaces are removed and words are joined. In camelCase, the first letter is lowercase and subsequent words are capitalized. In PascalCase, every word begins with a capital letter.
How does slugify work?＋
Slugify (or slug-case) replaces all spaces and special symbols with hyphens (dashes) and makes all letters lowercase. It is commonly used to generate SEO-friendly website URL structures (e.g. my-blog-post-title).
