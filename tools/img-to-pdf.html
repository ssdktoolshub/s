Frequently Asked Questions

Which formats are supported?＋
The tool supports standard formats including JPG/JPEG, PNG, WebP, BMP, and GIF. It uses browser rendering engines to handle conversions entirely client-side.
Does the PDF generation reduce image resolution?＋
By default, selecting "Fit to Image Dimensions" compiles the pages at the exact pixel resolution of your uploaded images. In A4 or Letter modes, the image scales to fit, preserving high definition.
