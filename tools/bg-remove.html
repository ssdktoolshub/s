Frequently Asked Questions

What type of backgrounds work best?＋
Chroma keying works best on images with solid, clean backgrounds (like green screens or solid studio backdrops) where there is a clear color contrast between the subject and the background.
How does eyedropper sampling work?＋
When you click on the image canvas, the tool samples the exact RGB value of the pixel under your cursor and sets it as the key color. This makes background extraction extremely fast for non-standard background colors.
