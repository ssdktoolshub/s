📚 Clinical Pathology Notes: Diabetes Risk Screening

How is the ADA Diabetes Risk Score designed?
The American Diabetes Association (ADA) risk test uses a point system to quickly identify asymptomatic individuals who may have prediabetes or undiagnosed Type 2 diabetes. A score of 5 or higher indicates that you are at high risk.

What role does BMI play?
Excess fat tissue, especially abdominal fat, increases insulin resistance. This means your pancreas has to work harder to produce insulin, eventually leading to exhaustion of insulin-producing cells and onset of Type 2 diabetes.
