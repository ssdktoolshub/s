Frequently Asked Questions

Is my file data secure during the merge?＋
Yes. Your PDF files do not get sent to any server. They are processed entirely within your computer's browser window, guaranteeing maximum data security and offline functionality.
Can I merge files with different page layouts or sizes?＋
Yes. The tool copies all dimensions, formats, and objects of each page directly from your source PDFs, ensuring that the merged file retains all layout properties.
