What is the ideal caption length?＋
For platforms like TikTok, short captions (1-2 sentences) are best as users focus on the video. For Instagram, a mix of short punchy captions and deeper narratives works well. For LinkedIn, structured, professional paragraphs outlining lessons learned drive the most engagement.
