📚 Clinical Notes: Urine Routine Analysis

What does a urine routine test include?
A standard urinalysis includes three components: (1) Physical examination — color, clarity, specific gravity; (2) Chemical dipstick analysis — pH, protein, glucose, ketones, bilirubin, urobilinogen, nitrite, leukocyte esterase, blood; (3) Microscopic examination — RBCs, WBCs, casts, crystals.

When is a urine routine test ordered?
Routine UTI screening, diabetes monitoring, kidney disease tracking, preoperative screening, pregnancy evaluation, and general health checkups.
