Frequently Asked Questions

What are analogous and triadic schemes?＋
Analogous colors are groups of three colors that are next to each other on the color wheel. Triadic colors are three colors that are evenly spaced around the color wheel, forming a triangle. Both offer different levels of visual contrast and harmony.
Can I export these color schemes into design tools?＋
Yes. By copying the CSS or JSON parameters, you can easily declare these variables inside web files or feed them into design software.
