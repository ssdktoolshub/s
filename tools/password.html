Key Features

  • Bulk Generation: Generate up to 50 passwords or passphrases at once and download as a .txt file.
  • Passphrase Mode: Combine random words from a 120-word built-in list with your choice of separator and capitalization.
  • Entropy Display: See exact bits of entropy so you know exactly how secure your password is.
  • Custom Characters: Add or exclude specific characters from the pool for compatibility with password policies.
  • Session History: Last 5 generated passwords kept in sessionStorage for quick recall — cleared when you close the tab.
  • Quick Presets: One-click PIN, Strong, WiFi Key, and Memorable presets that configure and generate instantly.
  • Local Execution: All generation happens in your browser. No passwords ever touch a network or server.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a password strong?＋
Length and variety. A strong password has 12+ characters and mixes uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. 80+ bits of entropy is considered very strong against modern attacks.
Are passphrases secure?＋
Yes — 5+ random words from a large wordlist can exceed 60 bits of entropy, making them highly resistant to brute force while being much easier to remember than random strings.
Are generated passwords recorded or transmitted?＋
No. The generator runs entirely inside your browser's memory. History is stored only in sessionStorage and is wiped when the tab closes. Nothing touches a network.
What does entropy mean?＋
Entropy measures unpredictability in bits. It's calculated as log₂(poolSize^length). More bits = more possible combinations = harder to crack. 60 bits is strong; 80+ is very strong.
