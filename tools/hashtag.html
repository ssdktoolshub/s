Frequently Asked Questions

How many hashtags should I use on Instagram?＋
Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. While some creators maximize this limit to capture wide searches, many social media managers recommend using **5 to 15 relevant, mixed-density hashtags** (combining popular, niche, and branded tags) to keep posts clean and target audiences precisely.
Why should I mix popular and niche hashtags?＋
Popular hashtags (like #travel) have massive search volumes but high competition, meaning your post gets buried instantly. Niche hashtags (like #travelbloggerslife) have lower search volume but highly engaged users, giving your content a better chance to rank in "Top Posts" and gain quality views.
